Privacy Policy for Razr VPN

Last updated: [April 28, 2026]

This Privacy Policy describes how Razr VPN ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application. By using Razr VPN, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

Razr VPN is designed with user privacy as a core principle. We collect the minimum information necessary to operate the service:

  • Connection data: Timestamp of connection, server location chosen, and amount of data transferred. This is used solely for maintaining service quality and is not tied to your browsing activity.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and app version, used for compatibility and crash diagnostics.
  • Anonymous usage analytics: Aggregated, non-identifying statistics about app feature usage.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

We do not log or store:

  • Your browsing history or websites visited
  • DNS queries
  • Traffic content passing through the VPN tunnel
  • Your originating IP address after the session ends

  • [e.g. Google Firebase Crashlytics — for crash reporting]

4. Permissions

Razr VPN requests the VPN service permission on your device, which is required to establish a secure tunnel. We do not request access to your contacts, photos, location, or other personal data.

5. Data Retention

Connection metadata is retained for a maximum of [7 days] for diagnostic purposes and is then automatically deleted. Anonymous analytics are retained in aggregate form only.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We will only disclose information if legally required by a valid legal process, and only data we actually have — which, by design, does not include your browsing activity.

7. Children's Privacy

Razr VPN is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
